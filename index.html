FuncyTag & FuncyStyle

Generate HTML/JS/CSS into nested Javascript functions.
FuncyTag & FuncyStyle allow all components of a web page (HTML, CSS, and Javascript) to be generated as purely functional source that still looks pretty much like standard HTML/CSS/JS. Easy to read, easy to write, easy to reuse. Source-code locality.

How are FuncyTag & FuncyStyle different?

We've crafted some handsome templates for you to use. Go ahead and continue to layouts to browse through them. You can easily go back to edit your page before publishing. After publishing your page, you can revisit the page generator and switch to another theme. Your Page content will be preserved if it remained markdown format.
